The Person Behind the Pixels

Having developed full-stack applications from the ground up, I have the unique ability to speak the language of engineering while using my Psychology background to advocate for the human experience. My perspective is shaped by 7 years of IT infrastructure and technical advisory, where I specialized in translating technical complexity into accessible user flows.

Currently, I am formalizing this intersection through an accelerated B.S./M.S. in Human Factors Psychology at Embry-Riddle. My goal is to apply rigorous human-centered design to high-stakes environments such as reducing cognitive load, eliminating use-errors, and building trust in the systems that define our future.
